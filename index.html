Welcome to Stockholm Traders, your trusted source for high-quality imported rice from the southern state of India.

We work closely with a network of experienced farmers and suppliers in the southern region to ensure that we bring you the finest rice grains available.

From aromatic Basmati rice to fragrant Jasmine rice, from traditional Sona Masoori to richly flavored Jeera rice, our diverse collection caters to every palate and culinary requirement. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to exceed your expectations in every aspect of our business.

we not only bring you the finest imported rice but also a taste of southern India's rich culinary heritage. Join us on this gastronomic journey as we bring the flavors of the south to your table and elevate your dining experience to new heights.

Trading

We purchase our food goods directly from Indian farmers in order to bring you only the best. Furthermore, we have successfully delivered and shipped a number of consignments in accordance with international standards. Since our founding, we have become a reputable company in the eyes of our clients.

Consulting

In addition to trading, we provide consulting services to provide efficient operations/logistics solutions. We provide a range of port services across Sweden for all types of cargo as well as acting as cargo agents. Our objective as a consultant is to provide expert advice and training that is based on needs and opportunities in the job market.
